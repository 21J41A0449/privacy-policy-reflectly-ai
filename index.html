Reflectly AI - Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-01-14

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide and improve our services. The information is stored securely on your device and is not uploaded to our servers unless explicitly stated.

a. Information You Provide:

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your email address. We do not collect your name or phone number.
  • User-Generated Content: This includes your mood tracking data, journal entries, manifestation entries, and reflections. This data is stored locally on your device in an encrypted database.

b. Information for AI Features:

  • AI Conversation History: To provide conversational context, your interactions with our AI features are processed. This data is sent to a third-party AI service (Google Gemini) to generate responses. We do not store this conversation history on our servers.

c. Information Collected Automatically:

  • Usage Analytics: We may collect anonymized data about how you interact with the app (e.g., feature usage, session duration) to help us improve the user experience. This data is not linked to your personal identity.
  • Crash Logs: If the app crashes, we may collect anonymized diagnostic information to help us identify and fix bugs. This may include device type and operating system version.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services
  • Secure your account and authenticate you
  • Process your inputs to provide AI-generated responses
  • Display your mood history and journal entries to you
  • Communicate with you about our services
  • Ensure platform security and prevent fraud
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Generate anonymized analytics for service improvement

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. Your data is not shared with third parties, except in the following limited circumstances:

  • With Third-Party AI Providers: Your inputs to AI features are sent to our AI service provider (Google) to generate responses. We encourage you to review Google's Privacy Policy.
  • With your explicit consent
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To protect our rights and prevent harm
  • In connection with a business transfer (e.g., a merger or acquisition), where we will notify you before your information is transferred.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information:

  • End-to-end encryption for sensitive data
  • Secure server infrastructure
  • Local Data Encryption: All your personal entries (moods, journals, etc.) are stored in an encrypted format on your device's local database.
  • Secure Communication: Communication with third-party AI services is encrypted using industry-standard protocols (TLS/SSL).
  • No Server-Side Storage: We do not store your personal journal entries, mood data, or AI conversations on our own servers, minimizing the risk of a centralized data breach.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations. You can request deletion of your data at any time through the app settings.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data stored within the app.
  • Correct inaccurate data (such as your email).
  • Delete your data. Deleting your account will permanently remove all associated data from our systems and your device.
  • Export your data (feature coming soon).
  • Object to data processing in certain circumstances.
  • Withdraw consent
  • Lodge a complaint with supervisory authorities

7. Cookies and Tracking

We may use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience. You can control cookie preferences through your device settings.

8. Third-Party Services

Our app integrates with Google Gemini to provide AI features. Your interactions with these features are subject to Google's Privacy Policy. We do not control how third-party services use your data and encourage you to review their policies.

9. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18. If we become aware that we have collected personal data from a minor without verification of parental consent, we take steps to remove that information.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes via email or app notification.
